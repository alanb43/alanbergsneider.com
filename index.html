Teaching

I had the privilege of becoming an Instructional Aide for EECS 495: Software Development for Accessibility starting fall semester 2022.

EECS 495 is a capstone for the computer science program at UMich, allowing students to work with a real client to develop software with accessibility as a central focus. I facilitate software development and act almost as a consultant to project teams, occasionally lecturing and leading discussion on software development topics.
